Zenshin Suru

Always getting better

"Zenshin suru" is a Japanese phrase that translates to "to advance" or "to progress" in English. It's often used in the context of personal development, improvement, or moving forward in some aspect of life or work. The term encapsulates a forward-moving attitude or approach, often associated with continual learning, growth, and adaptation.
